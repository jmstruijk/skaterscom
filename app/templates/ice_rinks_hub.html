What is an Ice Rink?

An ice rink is a frozen surface designed for ice skating activities. Ice rinks provide a smooth, maintained ice surface for various activities including recreational skating, ice hockey, figure skating, speed skating, and curling. Modern ice rinks use refrigeration systems to maintain consistent ice quality year-round, regardless of outdoor temperatures.

Ice rinks come in various sizes and configurations, from Olympic-sized competition venues to smaller community rinks. They can be indoor or outdoor, permanent or seasonal, and serve different purposes from professional sports to family entertainment.
